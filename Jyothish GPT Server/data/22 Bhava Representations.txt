Bhava representations are discussed here.
The first Bhava is called Thanu bhava and Thanu literally means body.
The second Bhava is called Dhana bhava and Dhana means wealth
The third Bhava is called Bhratru bhava and Bhratru means siblings
The fourth Bhava is called Matru bhava and Matru means mother
The fifth Bhava is called Putra bhva andPutra means children
The sixth Bhava is called Satru Bhava and Satru means enemies
The seventh Bhava is called Kalatrabhava and kalatra means Spouse
The eighth Bhava is called Ayurbhava and Ayur means Longevity
The ninth Bhava is called Pitrubhava and Pitru means father
The tenth Bhava is called Karma Bhava and karma Bhava literally denotes occupation
The eleventh Bhava is called Labha Bhava and Labha means Gains
The twelfth Bhava is called Vraya bhava and vraya means losses

The first Bhava represents Build, body, appearance, personality, mental characteristics, fame, success, livelihood, dignity, quality of life, and general strength of the horoscope.
The second Bhava represents wealth, financial, prosperity, family, face, speech, right eye, source of death, and banks and other financial institutions.
The third Bhava represents youngers brothers and sisters, intelligence, short journey, courage, right ear, throat, neck and shoulder, communication, etc,.
The fourth bhava represents mother, formal education, peace of mind, happiness, relatives, houses, and landed properties, vehicles, etc.
The fifth Bhava represents children, poorvapunya, that is past like credits, fame, luck, intelligence, emotions and feelings, creativity, speculations, etc.
The sixth Bhava represents enemies, thieves, disease wounds and cuts in the body, debts, miseries, disappointments etc.
The seventh Bhava represents spouse, marriage, marital happiness, urinary organs, sexual disease, trade, travel , business tact, honor, diplomacy, etc.
The eight the Bhava represents longevity, cause of death, impediments, disgrace, sorrow, sudden rise or fall etc.
The Ninth Bhava represents father, guru, grandchildren, institution, philosophy, lasting fame, luck, righteousness, leadership, charity, religious, devotion, long journey etc.
The tenth the Bhava represents occupation, rank, honors, success, respect, etc.
The Eleventh Bhava represents accomplishments, elder brother, Friends, freedom from misery, etc.
The twelfth Bhava represents losses, expenditures ,miseries, waste, sympathy, final emancipation(Moksha) etc.
