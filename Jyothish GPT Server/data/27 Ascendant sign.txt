Ascendant sign is discussed here
The horoscope is the map of heavenly bodies during birth of the child. To analyze a horoscope We need a reference point to start our analysis and this reference point is called the Ascendant sign.
During birth of the child the zodiac sign in which Sun occupy is called the Sun sign
During birth of the child, the zodiac sign in which the moon occupy is called the Moon sign.
The Ascendant sign is the sign which is rising in the east during birth of the child. Since this ascendant sign is changing every two hours this is the best reference point to start the analysis in the horoscope.
